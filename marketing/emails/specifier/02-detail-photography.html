The details, close up.

Render images show intent. Workshop photographs show capability. Here is what our production floor looks like at the detail level — the joints, finishes and edge profiles that define the quality of a completed interior.

Veneer matching

Book-matched panels laid out, inspected and approved before lamination. We control grain direction and colour across every sheet so a 40-metre wall reads as one surface.

CNC joinery

Mortise-and-tenon joints machined to 0.1 mm tolerance, then hand-finished. The precision is invisible in the final piece — which is the point.

Surface finishing

Multi-coat lacquer systems applied in controlled conditions. Sheen levels matched to specification, tested against your sample board before production runs.

Fire-rated assemblies

Door leaves and frames tested to BS 476 / EN 1634. Certification documentation prepared per-unit for handover packs.
