See it made.

Renders and samples tell part of the story. A factory visit tells the rest. We host architects, interior designers and specifiers at our Istanbul production facility throughout the year — it is the fastest way to understand what we can deliver for your project.

What a visit includes

Production walkthrough. CNC machining, veneer lay-up, lacquer lines, assembly and QC — you see every stage that a piece of furniture moves through.

Material library. Timber species, veneer books, stone, metal finishes, fabric and leather sourcing — review the palette we work with and discuss what your project needs.

Active projects on the floor. See real production runs at various stages — joinery panels in clamps, casegoods in finishing, furniture in final assembly.

Technical conversation. Our production engineers walk you through tolerances, lead times, finish options, and how your specification translates to our manufacturing process.
