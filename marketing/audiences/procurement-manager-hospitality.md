# Procurement manager at a hospitality developer running fit-out tenders

**Segment:** Procurement teams (gatekeeper)
**Status:** draft
**Weight:** 7

## Role & firmographics

- Procurement manager or contracts lead at a hospitality group or large developer
- Manages the tender/RFP process for interior fit-out packages across multiple projects
- Based in GCC or Türkiye; works with international and regional contractors
- KPIs: cost compliance, vendor qualification, on-time delivery, contract risk reduction

## Jobs to be done

1. Qualify vendors for tenders: confirm scale, certifications, insurance, and project references
2. Compare like-for-like across 3–5 bidders using a structured RFP response
3. Ensure the selected contractor can deliver across the full scope (joinery, FF&E, doors) to reduce contract fragmentation
4. Protect the organisation from risk — warranties, defect-liability, performance bonds

## Pains

- Contractors whose websites don't clearly state capabilities, certifications, or project scale
- RFP responses that are marketing brochures instead of structured answers
- Splitting scope across multiple contractors increases coordination cost and contract risk
- Difficulty verifying claims — "200+ projects" means nothing without named references

## Objections

- "Your service taxonomy doesn't match our RFP line items — can you map your scope to ours?"
- "We need fire-test certificates, acoustic test reports, and ISO documentation upfront."
- "Can you provide three named references in our sector and region?"

## Buying triggers

- New project entering tender stage — RFP issued or shortlist being compiled
- Board mandate to consolidate the preferred-supplier panel
- Previous contractor failed audit or performance review

## Vocabulary

- RFP, ITT, PQQ, pre-qualification
- Scope of works, bill of quantities, preliminaries
- Performance bond, retention, defect-liability period
- ISO 9001, fire test certificate, acoustic test report
- Preferred supplier list, framework agreement, approved vendor

## Content that resonates

- About page with quality standards, certifications, and global presence
- Service taxonomy that maps cleanly to RFP line items
- Named project references with scale data (room count, sqm, timeline)
- Downloadable capability statement or company profile PDF
