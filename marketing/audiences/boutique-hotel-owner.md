# Independent boutique hotel owner renovating a heritage property

**Segment:** Hospitality principals (hotel owners, hospitality groups, developers)
**Status:** draft
**Weight:** 7

## Role & firmographics

- Owner-operator of 1–3 boutique hotels (20–80 rooms each)
- Based in Türkiye or southern Europe; property is often a heritage conversion
- Personally involved in design decisions; works with a small architecture practice
- Budget-conscious but quality-driven — the property *is* the brand

## Jobs to be done

1. Deliver bespoke interiors that express the property's character — not chain-hotel generic
2. Navigate heritage constraints (listed buildings, conservation requirements) with a contractor who adapts
3. Hit a seasonal opening window — missing summer costs an entire year of revenue
4. Get turnkey delivery without managing multiple trades personally

## Pains

- Heritage buildings surprise: uneven walls, non-standard dimensions, hidden structural issues
- Small scale means less negotiating power with large contractors — feels deprioritised
- Past experiences with contractors who couldn't adapt joinery to site conditions
- Limited internal project management capacity — needs a partner, not just a vendor

## Objections

- "Your portfolio is mostly large hospitality — will you give my 40-room project proper attention?"
- "Heritage work needs flexibility. Can your factory handle one-off dimensions?"
- "I need to see and touch materials before committing — can you do a mock-up room?"

## Buying triggers

- Planning permission granted for a heritage conversion
- Existing property undergoing renovation with a hard seasonal deadline
- Architect has completed design development and needs a fit-out contractor

## Vocabulary

- Heritage conversion, conservation officer, listed building
- Bespoke joinery, site-measured, templated
- Mock-up room, sample panel, material board
- Opening season, soft opening, phased handover

## Content that resonates

- Case studies showing bespoke/heritage work — adaptive reuse, custom joinery
- Manufacturing flexibility stories — one-off pieces alongside series production
- Process transparency: 7-step delivery with clear milestones
- Testimonials from owner-operators (not just corporate clients)
