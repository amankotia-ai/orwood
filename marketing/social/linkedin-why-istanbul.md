When a hospitality developer in Doha or Dubai shortlists fit-out partners, most have offices in the Gulf. What often goes unexamined is where the furniture, joinery and doors are actually made.

For many, the answer is the same: Turkey. The difference is whether the firm owns the factory or simply places an order with one.

ORWOOD has manufactured in Istanbul since 2004. Three owned facilities — Hi Mobilya for furniture, SILADU for materials, ORWOOD Doors for fire-rated and acoustic door systems — staffed by our own machinists, joiners and finishing teams.

Istanbul's advantage is structural: a deep craft tradition layered with modern CNC production, two hours from Dubai, four from London, shipping to the Gulf in days.

The prototype you approve is the product you receive, at scale.
