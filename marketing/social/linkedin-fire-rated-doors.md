A fire-rated door is not a commodity line item. It is an assembly — leaf, frame, seals, hardware and certification — that must work together or fail together.

Change the closer. Swap the frame profile. Source the seals from a different manufacturer. The certification may no longer apply. The door still looks like a fire-rated door. It may not perform like one when it matters.

For a 300-key hotel with 400+ rated openings, the cost of getting this wrong — failed inspections, programme delays, post-opening remediation — dwarfs any upfront saving.

ORWOOD Doors are engineered, manufactured and tested as a complete set in our Istanbul facilities. The test reports cover the assembly as built, not a theoretical combination of parts from different suppliers.

When the authority reviews the documentation, the installed product matches the tested product.
