The luxury hospitality industry runs on a fragmented supply chain.

A design studio draws the interior. A procurement house sources the furniture. A joinery contractor makes the millwork. A fit-out firm installs. A separate specialist supplies the doors. At each handoff, intent leaks.

Delayed openings, mismatched finishes and last-minute substitutions are not design failures. They are supply-chain failures — solved by owning the supply chain.

At ORWOOD, design, engineering and manufacture happen in the same building. Hi Mobilya for furniture. SILADU for materials. ORWOOD Doors for fire-rated and acoustic systems. The designer who specified a finish can walk onto the factory floor to approve it.

One contract. One programme. One team responsible for the gap between what was drawn and what was built.
